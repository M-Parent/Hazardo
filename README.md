# Hazardo

**Can't decide what to do? Roll the dice.**

Hazardo is a random activity picker that helps you choose what to do based on your mood, time of day, and who you're with. Build your own vault of activities, and let Hazardo surprise you.

## How It Works

1. **Build your vault** — Add activities to categories (restaurants, outdoor, movies, games, etc.). 50+ categories are included by default.
2. **Set your preferences** — Pick your time of day (AM / PM / Night) and your vibe (Friend / Date / Family).
3. **Roll the dice** — Hazardo randomly picks an activity matching your criteria.
4. **Track your picks** — Every pick is saved with the date, location, weather, and you can add notes and photos.

## Features

- **Random Picker** — Filter by time, vibe, and category, then roll
- **Activity Vault** — Organize activities in custom categories
- **Pick History** — Notes, photos, location & weather for every pick
- **AI Chatbot** — Ask for activity suggestions (supports Ollama, OpenAI, Gemini)
- **Import / Export** — Backup and share your vault via JSON, CSV, Markdown, or ZIP
- **Multi-user** — Multiple profiles on one device
- **Bilingual** — English & French
- **Dark Mode** — Light & dark themes
